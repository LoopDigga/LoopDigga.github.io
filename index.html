Bio

Carl Sagan was a famous astrophysicist that helped to spread scientific literacy through his various award winning books and television series'. Some of Sagan's favorite activities involved looking at the stars, picking up honies at the mall, and astro traveling in/to different locations. Carl Sagan was a man who believed the most in the power of humankind to achieve great things, rather than on any deity. "Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known." -Carl Sagan
